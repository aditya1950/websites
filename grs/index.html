Is there a momentum effect for baseball teams going into the playoffs?

The aim of this assignment is to calculate the momentum of the team, i.e. Predict if the team will perform well in the next season by evaluating the performance of the teams in the selected season. To proceed with the calculations we first selected year 1998 for which we will evaluate the performance of each pairings for all the series. Here we populated 3 data series in the graph which were for the following:
For September month
For the overall season
For the half season
 To calculate the data point of the above series we calculated 'x' by subtracting 'how hot the loosing team performed' by 'how hot the winning team performed'. However for the 'y' values the points by which the winning team won the series were considered. This was done for all the values of the above mentioned data series. The graph that was plotted was a scattered graph which then used to calculate the momentum of team for the next season.

In conlusion,according to calculations presented above we see that the team NYY and ATL have momentum to win in the next season i.e year 1999. Now we have the results for the next few years, so it shows that team NYY have kept the winning streak alive for next 2 consecutive years. Also team ATL reached the finals in 1999 against NYY, but unfortunately lost. In addition, NYY reached the finals in 2001. This result gives a brief idea that, yes it is possible that winning momentum makes the teams do better in the post season
